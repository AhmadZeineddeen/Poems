I’ve made up my mind, I am going to sweep these feelings under the rug

Despite the pain it is going to leave or the huge pit that it had dug

A pit that cannot be sealed, filled or closed by a plug

But at least now if someone asks me do you like her I wouldn’t just shrug

I wouldn’t say that I am still waiting for the day I confess and her giving me a hug

I wouldn’t say that my love for her made me feel numb like some type of drug

I wouldn’t say that my heart was full of words just like water filling up a jug

Yes, it is time to pack the feeling stuck in my heart that I thought no one can tug

The feeling that I thought at first was smug
